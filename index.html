Our Story

A family name on every job — for over 65 years.

Since 1958, the Blakley family has kept Fort Wayne's water running clean and its homes dry. What started as one trusted plumber has grown into a small, dedicated team that still answers the phone, shows up on time, and treats your home like their own.

We're known for understanding old homes — the quirks, the original fittings, the surprises behind the wall — and for explaining exactly what's going on before any work begins. No upselling. No mess left behind. Just honest, skilled work and pricing you can trust.
